Background

Identity V: 1 vs 4 Asymmetrical Horror Mobile Game
Welcome to Identity V,
the first asymmetrical horror mobile game developed by NetEase.
With a gothic art style,
mysterious storylines and exciting 1vs4 gameplay,
Identity V will bring you a breathtaking experience.
You will enter the game as detective first,
who receives a mysterious letter being invited to
investigate an abandoned manor and search for a missing girl.
As you close to the truth,
you will discover increasingly incredible realities.
