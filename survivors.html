Lucky Guy

Lucky Guy specializes in versatility, able to manually select certain items from a chest (increasing his odds of receiving it) and always having the right tool for the job. Additionally, he lacks a debuff, making him a relatively low-risk character. His decoding speed is normal/average.
His world is like a slot machine that he always wins at. But who knows what will happen when he stops playing. Besides some basic information, nothing is known about Lucky Guy, not even his real name. He is right-handed and of average height.


Interests: Walking
Talent: Scavenging
Likes: Box

Lawyer

Since a botched lawsuit, Freddy has been toiling away at a menial job with a pathetic wage. But he hopes to find a way to live the life he has never had: he dreams of receiving a huge bonus, or becoming the upper class. Of course, first he needs to find the culprit who ruined his perfect life.
The Lawyer is a decode-type Survivor who carries around a Map with limited durability, which enables him to see the outlines of the Hunter, his teammates, and nearby Cipher Machines clearly. He cannot be Terror Shocked and is one of two decoders who don't have any debuffs.

Interest: Reading, Writing
Talent: Writing, Negotiating
Likes: Martha, Money

Female Dancer

Margaretha is a gorgeous dancer and is used to the good life. After divorcing from her estranged husband and without stable income, Margaretha has gained a new understanding of "freedom". How could she pass up an opportunity to become a millionaire?
The Female Dancer is a contain/assist-type Survivor who carries up to three Music Boxes. She can change the tune of the Music Box to either make characters' actions slower or faster. While within the music box's range, she can perform up to 2 pirouettes to gain distance.

Interests: Parties, Shopping
Talent: Dancing, Acrobatics impersonation
Likes: Jewelry, fine clothes, luggage

Cowboy

Kevin Ayuso, a cowboy skilled with a lasso, befriended a Native American girl in his youth. Later saved by her tribe after misfortune, he lived with them until their demise. With no reason to stay, he set off to roam the European continent, seeking purpose and adventure.
Kevin can lasso other survivors toward him, and he can carry them to safety. He can also lasso the hunter and flip over them. The lasso can be used on pallets and cipher machines, and will pull him towards these objects.


Interests: Camping, Hunting
Talent: Lassoing, Herding
Likes: Hunting goods, Weapons

Embalmer

Aesop Carl, meticulous in guiding final journeys with utmost respect, discovers a letter on a mysterious mother. Determined to honor her last wish, he takes her place, ensuring her journey is completed with the same care he shows to all.
The Embalmer is an assist-type Survivor who is able to summon a Coffin which can used for rescuing both himself and his teammates from being downed or chaired by the Hunter and teleport them to the Coffin and grant them invincibility from any damage from the Hunter for 7 seconds.


Interests: Being alone
Talent: Funeral
Likes: Dead people

Cheerleader

Lily Barriere, from a wealthy sports family, faced tragedy at 16 when a horse racing accident left her brother disabled and her family in ruins. Abandoned by relatives, she relied on her grandfather's limited support and an IOU tied to the mysterious Oletus Manor.
The Cheerleader is an assist/contain-type Survivor who possesses the ability to give only herself, or both her and her teammates speed boosts using Motivate/Encourage. As a downside, her decoding speed is reduced by 10%.
